About Me

Researcher. Educator. Builder.

Temitayo Elijah Balogun is a lecturer in the Information Systems Department at the Federal University of Technology, Akure (FUTA). He holds a B.Tech, M.Tech, and PhD in Computer Science from the same institution — and graduated in the top 1% of his master's class.

A machine learning expert, web developer, and researcher, he is currently working on multiple research grants. His research spans data science, information security systems, open computer vision, and web systems development. He has authored and co-authored numerous high-indexed publications.

Beyond academia, Temitayo is a prolific community builder — managing large online communities, training people in data science fundamentals, and volunteering with organisations dedicated to youth digital empowerment.
